# Contributing to Skillception

Thank you for your interest in contributing to Skillception!

## How to Contribute

1. Fork the repository
2. Create a feature branch (`git checkout -b my-feature`)
3. Make your changes
4. Submit a pull request

## Developer Certificate of Origin

By submitting a pull request, you certify that:

1. Your contribution is your own original work, or you have the right to submit it under the project's MIT license.
2. You grant the project the right to distribute your contribution under the MIT license.
3. You understand your contribution will be publicly available.

## Questions?

If you have questions, open an issue or contact the maintainer before contributing.
